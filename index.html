Original, Funky & Heartfelt

Original songs with tales of life, love and longing,interwoven with a smooth, funky South American groove.

Edel and Noni met while studying music 20 years ago. They have released 2 albums 'As is' in 2005 and 'Unravel' in 2015. edel on vox, ukulele and guitar and Noni on backing vox and guitar.

Using both Irish and Spanish words for friend (cara & amigo) the name Caramigo was born.
